OVAL FACE
Characteristics:
Balanced proportions. Slightly longer than wide. Forehead slightly wider than jaw. Considered the "ideal" face shape.

Best Hairstyles:

Pompadour with volume on top

Deep side part with texture

Textured crop with messy top

Slight wave/fringe over forehead

Slick back with gelled volume

Hairstyles to Avoid:

Heavy bangs covering forehead

Too short losing face-framing

Flat center part

Beard Styles:
Any style works! Try goatee, stubble, full beard, or mustache.

Eyebrows:
Natural shape with slight arch.

Best Glasses:

Square frames for definition

Rectangular frames extending width

Aviator for symmetry

Cat-eye for playful look

Glasses to Avoid:

Round frames (look boring)

Oversized (overwhelms balance)

SQUARE FACE
Characteristics:
Strong, broad forehead. Wide jawline. Forehead, cheeks, and jaw same width. Angular features.

Best Hairstyles:

Textured crop with short sides

Side swept bangs breaking forehead

Pompadour with height on top

Medium length shoulder waves

Messy quiff with texture

Hairstyles to Avoid:

Buzz cut emphasizing squareness

Center part dividing and widening

Flat top making face boxy

Beard Styles:

Goatee with mustache adds length

Circle beard softens jaw

Stubble maintains definition

Eyebrows:
Rounded arch to soften features.

Best Glasses:

Round frames soften angles

Oval counteracts squareness

Aviator adds softness

Glasses to Avoid:

Square makes face boxier

Angular emphasizes squareness

ROUND FACE
Characteristics:
Width and height similar. Full cheeks. Soft, curved lines. No sharp angles.

Best Hairstyles:

Pompadour with tall volume

Deep side part creating asymmetry

Textured crop with disconnected top

Slick back with height on top

Faux hawk with volume in center

Hairstyles to Avoid:

Center part widening face

Full bangs shortening face

Too short adding width

Beard Styles:

Goatee with soul patch elongates chin

Extended goatee creates point

Anchor beard defines jaw

Eyebrows:
Angled arch to lengthen face.

Best Glasses:

Square adds angles

Rectangular elongates face

Geometric breaks roundness

Glasses to Avoid:

Round mirrors face shape

Oval too similar to shape

HEART FACE
Characteristics:
Wide forehead. High cheekbones. Narrow chin. Iconic "angel" face shape.

Best Hairstyles:

Side swept bangs covering forehead

Chin-length bob adding width at bottom

Soft layers distributing volume

Wavy shoulder length waves

Deep side part creating balance

Hairstyles to Avoid:

Volume at top emphasizing forehead

Center part widening forehead

Pixie cut exposing all width

Beard Styles:

Full goatee adds chin width

Van Dyke elongates chin

Circle beard gives fullness at jaw

Eyebrows:
Arched but not too high.

Best Glasses:

Round softens forehead

Oval gives balanced look

Rimless light and open

Glasses to Avoid:

Cat-eye emphasizes width

Heavy top adds weight to forehead

OBLONG FACE
Characteristics:
Longer than wide. Deep, prominent forehead. Narrow jaw. Large forehead and chin.

Best Hairstyles:

Long bangs across forehead

Layered cut with volume on sides

Medium length shoulder waves

Curly/wavy adding fullness

Deep side part adding width

Hairstyles to Avoid:

Long hair straight (elongates)

Top volume (adds height)

Center part (divides length)

Beard Styles:

Full beard adds jaw width

Stubble defines shape

Mustache adds width

Eyebrows:
Straight and flat (avoid high arch).

Best Glasses:

Square adds width

Oversized broadens face

Wayfarer shortens appearance

Glasses to Avoid:

Narrow frames (elongates)

Oval frames (emphasizes length)
