OLIVE SKIN
Characteristics:
Warm undertone with greenish/yellowish hue. Tans easily, rarely burns. Common in Mediterranean, South Asian, and Latin American ethnicities.

Skincare Routine:

Cleanse: Gentle foaming cleanser

Tone: Rose water or glycolic acid

Treat: Vitamin C serum (morning)

Moisturize: Lightweight gel moisturizer

Protect: SPF 50+ (tinted)

Recommended Products:

Cleanser: CeraVe Foaming Cleanser

Vitamin C: The Ordinary Vitamin C Suspension 23%

Moisturizer: Neutrogena Hydro Boost Water Gel

Sunscreen: Supergoop Unseen Sunscreen SPF 40

Night Cream: La Roche-Posay Toleriane Night

Makeup Shades:

Foundation: Warm yellow/golden undertones

Concealer: Peach/Orange corrector for dark circles

Blush: Terracotta, warm peach, brick red

Lipstick: Nude brown, warm red, mauve

Eyeshadow: Gold, bronze, olive green, warm brown

Avoid:

Ash-toned foundations

Cool pink blushes

Frosted lipsticks

WHITE/FAIR SKIN
Characteristics:
Cool/pink undertone. Burns easily, freckles common. Common in Northern European ethnicities.

Skincare Routine:

Cleanse: Creamy milk cleanser

Tone: Hydrating toner with ceramides

Treat: Niacinamide serum

Moisturize: Rich cream with ceramides

Protect: SPF 50+ (mineral)

Recommended Products:

Cleanser: La Roche-Posay Toleriane Hydrating Cleanser

Serum: The Ordinary Niacinamide 10% + Zinc 1%

Moisturizer: CeraVe Daily Moisturizing Cream

Sunscreen: EltaMD UV Clear SPF 46

Night Cream: Kiehl's Ultra Facial Cream

Makeup Shades:

Foundation: Pink/neutral undertones

Concealer: Brightening, peachy for under eyes

Blush: Soft pink, peach, cool rose

Lipstick: Rose, berry, soft mauve, red with blue undertones

Eyeshadow: Soft browns, taupes, rose gold, muted plum

Avoid:

Yellow/olive foundations

Deep orange bronzers

Dark brown lipsticks

DARK BROWN SKIN
Characteristics:
Warm/red undertone. Deep, rich pigmentation. Common in African, Afro-Caribbean, and South Indian ethnicities.

Skincare Routine:

Cleanse: Creamy cleanser

Tone: Acid toner (salicylic)

Treat: Alpha Arbutin or Kojic Acid

Moisturize: Rich shea butter cream

Protect: SPF 50+ (sheer/invisible)

Recommended Products:

Cleanser: Black Girl Sunscreen Hydrating Cleanser

Serum: Good Molecules Discoloration Correcting Serum

Moisturizer: SheaMoisture Manuka Honey Cream

Sunscreen: Black Girl Sunscreen SPF 50

Night Cream: Fenty Skin Butta Drop

Makeup Shades:

Foundation: Red/golden undertones

Concealer: Orange/red corrector for dark circles

Blush: Deep berry, brick, terracotta, purple

Lipstick: Deep red, berry, brown, wine, nude

Eyeshadow: Gold, copper, blue, purple, deep green

Avoid:

Ashy/grey foundations

Pale pink blushes

Frosted eye shadows
